ABOUT

I am a kid from Texas, whose never-ending questions of "why?" and "how?" (and also "???") took me on a journey around the world where I wore many hats and walked in many people's shoes.

I am also a product person and a developer, although some of you may know me from my former work as an operations manager, energy analyst, international development consultant, teacher, and writer. I love the holistic experience of building and launching a product, where every discipline (those hats came in handy!) comes together to produce something valuable.

Understanding diverse perspectives drives me. Getting out of a comfort zone motivates me. I never want to stop learning. My skills were sharpened at MIT Sloan (MBA), UT Austin (BBA), Hack Reactor (software immersion program), The Energy Resources Institute (renewable energy engineering), and the internets.

My tools of choice are full-stack Javascript (React, Node/Express, Angular), SQL and NoSQL (PostgresSQL, MongoDB), Git, Balsamiq, InVision, and Sketch. I'm familar with systems design for scalability and service-oriented architecture. Product Hunt is my homepage.

More? Check out my portfolio or LinkedIn.
